# ⏪ ClipBack — instant replay for your phone screen

Something funny, weird, or important just happened on your phone — and it's gone.
**ClipBack** quietly keeps the **last 10 seconds** of your screen in memory at all times.
Tap **Save** and it's a video in your gallery. Miss nothing, record nothing you didn't ask for.

## How it works
- Tap **Start ClipBack** once. Android asks permission to view your screen — that's the
  standard system prompt, and a recording icon stays visible while ClipBack runs.
- From then on, the last 10 seconds are always ready. **Nothing is written to storage**
  until you tap **⏪ Save last 10 seconds** (in the app or right from the notification).
- Saved clips land in your gallery under **Movies/ClipBack**.
- Stop anytime from the app or the notification. Stopping wipes the memory buffer.

## Privacy
The rolling 10-second buffer lives only in the phone's memory, is overwritten
continuously, and is never uploaded anywhere. The app has **no internet permission**.

## Install (Android)
Grab **ClipBack.apk** from the [latest release](../../releases/latest), open it on your
phone, and allow the install. Android 10 or newer.
