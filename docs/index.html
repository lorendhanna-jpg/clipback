How it works

1 · Start it once

Approve the standard screen-capture permission and put your phone away. A visible notification shows whenever ClipBack is watching.

2 · Save from anywhere

The moment happens in any app — swipe down and tap "Save last 10 seconds" in the notification. No app switching.

3 · It's a normal video

Clips land in your gallery under Movies/ClipBack. Watch, trim, share.

Built to be trusted

🔒 No internet, period

ClipBack has no internet permission at all — it is physically unable to upload anything, ever.

🧠 Memory only

The rolling buffer lives in RAM and is overwritten continuously. Nothing touches storage until you tap Save. Stop ClipBack and it's gone.

👁 Always visible

A recording indicator and a persistent notification show whenever it's watching. No stealth mode — by design.
